OPD runs Monday to Saturday — mornings from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and evenings from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Individual doctors have their own consultation hours, listed on our Contact page.

Yes. Gynaec, obstetric and pediatric emergency facilities are available 24 hours a day, every day — including nights, Sundays and holidays.

Yes. Our hospital has a fully equipped labour room and operation theatre, and our obstetricians manage both normal and complicated deliveries, with 24-hour emergency backup.

Yes. We have a NICU (newborn intensive care unit) and a phototherapy unit for newborn jaundice, along with a consultant pediatrician, Dr. Piyush B. Patel (M.B.B.S., M.D., D.C.H.), and 24 Hrs pediatric emergency support.

Yes. Vardhaman Hospital has a dedicated infertility treatment centre with IUI (intrauterine insemination). Our gynaecologists will first evaluate both partners and then recommend the most suitable treatment path.

We perform hysterectomy (vaginal, abdominal and laparoscopic), diagnostic & operative laparoscopy and hysteroscopy, laparotomy and laparoscopic tubal ligation. Surgeries are performed by our gynaecologists along with Dr. Pradeep V. Mistry, a general & laparoscopic surgeon with 43 years of experience.

Yes, sonography is available in-house — for pregnancy monitoring as well as gynaecological diagnosis — so you don't need to visit a separate centre.

We provide care for girls, adolescents, expecting mothers and women through menopause and later-life health concerns. Pediatric services are available for children who need newborn or child health support.

Yes, our vaccination centre provides immunisation for newborns and children, as well as vaccines recommended for women at different stages of life.
